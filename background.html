Leadership

Meet the visionary leading our mission to transform mental healthcare

Dr. Sarah Johnson

CEO & Founder

We're building technology that understands human emotions as well as humans do, but can provide support at scale to millions of people simultaneously.

With over 15 years of experience at the intersection of AI and mental health research, Dr. Johnson holds a Ph.D. in Cognitive Neuroscience from Stanford University and previously led AI research teams at Google Brain and DeepMind Health.

Her groundbreaking work on emotion recognition algorithms has been featured in Nature, Science, and The New England Journal of Medicine. Under her leadership, Viora AI has secured $75M in funding and established partnerships with major healthcare providers worldwide.

Our Journey

2020

Company Founded

Dr. Sarah Johnson establishes Viora AI with seed funding of $5M to develop emotion recognition technology for mental health applications.

2021

First AI Model Launch

Release of our proprietary emotion recognition AI with 95% accuracy, establishing new industry benchmarks for sentiment analysis.

2022

Global Expansion

Viora AI expands to 15+ countries and receives FDA approval for our mental health monitoring system. Series B funding round raises $40M.

2023

Advanced AI Integration

Launch of comprehensive mental health monitoring and support system with real-time intervention capabilities. User base grows to 250,000+.

2024

Reaching New Heights

Expanded to 45+ countries with 500,000+ users. Released multimodal AI system that integrates voice, facial, and text analysis for holistic support.
